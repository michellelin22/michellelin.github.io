Welcome to my website!

Hi everyone! My name is Michelle Lin and I am currently a senior at Northeastern University studying Business Administration with a concentration in Finance and a minor in Data Science. Throughout my time at Northeastern, I have had the opportunity to gain valuable professional experience through two co-op positions. I worked at Scotiabank in New York City, where I gained exposure to financial operations and the global banking environment, and later at Bain Capital in Boston, MA, where I was able to further develop my analytical and problem-solving skills in a fast-paced investment firm setting. These experiences helped me better understand how financial institutions operate and strengthened my interest in financial markets and strategy. In addition to my professional experience, I also studied abroad in Madrid, Spain last semester. During that time, I was able to travel to seven different countries, which gave me the opportunity to experience new cultures, meet people from around the world, and develop a more global perspective. On campus, I am actively involved in organizations that support students pursuing careers in finance. I serve as a peer mentor for Women in Finance, where I help guide underclassmen who are exploring career opportunities in the industry. I was also a Research Director for GMA, where I led a team of analysts in conducting market research and producing reports on industry trends. These experiences have helped me build leadership, teamwork, and communication skills that I hope to continue developing in my future career.
