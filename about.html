My Interests

Some of my favorite memories have come from visiting new cities and exploring places I have never been before. Photography is another hobby I really enjoy because it allows me to capture meaningful moments and document experiences, especially when I am traveling or spending time with friends. Taking photos helps me appreciate small details and preserve memories that I can look back on later. Most importantly, I value spending time with friends and building strong relationships with the people around me. Whether it is trying new restaurants, traveling together, or simply spending time catching up, those moments are what make life the most meaningful to me.
